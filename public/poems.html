Poems

BY- Edgar Allan Poe (1809-1849)

Take this kiss upon the brow!
And, in parting from you now,
Thus much let me avow—
You are not wrong, who deem
That my days have been a dream:
Yet if hope has flown away
In a night, or in a day,
In a vision or in none,
Is it therefore the less gone?
All that we see or seem
Is but a dream within a dream.

I stand amid the roar
Of a surf-tormented shore,
And I hold within my hand
Grains of the golden sand—
How few! yet how they creep
Through my fingers to the deep
While I weep—while I weep!
O God! can I not grasp
Them with a tighter clasp?
O God! can I not save
One from the pitiless wave?
Is all that we see or seem
But a dream within a dream?
We sit and we type and we stare at our screens,
We can’t help but wonder what all of this means.
With mouse in hand …we roam through this maze,
On an infinite search…lost in a daze.
We chat with each other, we type all our woes
At times we’ll band together to gang up on our foes.
We wait for somebody, to type out our name
We want recognition, but it is always the same.
Soon friendships are formed - but - why we don’t know,
But some of these friendships, will flourish and grow.
We give kisses and hugs, and sometimes we’ll flirt,
In IMs we chat deeply, and reveal why we hurt.
Why is it on screen, we are so easily bold,
Telling our secrets, that have never been told.
The answer is simple, it is as clear as a bell,
We all have our problems, and need someone to tell.
We can’t tell real people, but tell someone we must
So we turn to our ‘puters …and to those we can trust.
Even though it sounds crazy…the truth still remains,
Most of my “friends” have no faces…and odd little names.
A moment in time… to be my friend,
To show how much you care,
To make me laugh uncontrollably, even when I didn’t want to.
A moment in time…..to allow me to just…be me,
To have the most random conversations, that only make sense to us,
To lend me your ear when I had burdens or concerns.
A moment in time…..to win my heart, when I didn’t think it was possible,
To show me I am deserving of someone else’s love and affections.
A moment in time…. to reach out, when I thought all things were lost between us,
To let me know that you will always be in my life, no matter what happens,
To make me realize that all it ever takes to show you care about someone is
A moment in time…
It’s you, who brings the sunshine to my eyes,
Even on the most cloudy days.
It’s you, who brings a smile to my face
By just sending me the most random messages.
It’s you, who brings the uncontrollable laughter,
That seems so infectious that everyone wants to join in.
It’s you, who makes my heart skip a beat
Just at the mere mention of your name.
It’s you, who brings butterflies to my stomach,
With endless thoughts about possiblilites to come.
It’s you, who makes me weak in the knees,
By you just being your usual charming, goofy self.
